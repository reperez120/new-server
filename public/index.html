Rachel Perez's Photo Collection

Below is a collection of some photos that I took of plants and landscapes, in no special order.

The focus of this collection is a reflection of how I enjoy being outdoors. In displaying these images, I hope to share some of the enjoyment they gave me, and demonstrate a small piece of the beauty of the natural world, which is well worth preserving.
